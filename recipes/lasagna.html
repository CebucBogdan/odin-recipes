Information about Lasagna

Lasagna is a culinary dish made with stacked layers of pasta alternated with sauces and ingredients such as meats, vegetables and cheese, and sometimes topped with melted grated mozzarella cheese.

It is made with a type of wide, flat pasta, possibly one of the oldest types of pasta.

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 28 ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water
